Parable of the Sower

Octavia Butler

Parable of the Sower and its sequel, Parable of the Talents, follow the journey of Lauren Olamina as she escapes a plague causing people to kill each other. All while having hyperempathy that forces her to feel the pain of every person she sees. Lauren and I have the same birthday and the book begins on our shared birthday this year: July 20th, 2024. I personally believe that Octavia Butler left us so many keys and clues in this book; observations of our society and the cycles we're bound to repeat.

Slaughterhouse Five

Kurt Vonnegut

Kurt Vonnegut is the REALEST author I have ever read. He is often discounted as an easy read and I agree that his style of writing is very easy to follow.
